public initialize(options={},&block)

Creates a new Author object with the given options. If block is given, it will be passed the newly created Author object.

info may contain the following keys:

:name:The name of the author. Defaults to ANONYMOUSE.
:organization:The organization of the author.
:pgp_signature:The PGP signature of the author.
:email:The email address of the author.
:url:The URL for the author.
:biography:The biography of the author.
