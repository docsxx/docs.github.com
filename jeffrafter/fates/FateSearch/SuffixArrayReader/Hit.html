Class: FateSearch::SuffixArrayReader::Hit

  • Struct
    • FateSearch::SuffixArrayReader::Hit

A hit is a structure that represents single match for a term. The structure contains the term, the index of the term in the suffix array, the offset of the term in the fulltext and the fulltext reader.
