Class: Reve::Classes::IndustryJob

Used in Reve::API#personal_industry_jobs and Reve::API#corporate_industry_jobs. PersonalIndustryJob and CorporateIndustryJob subclass this for more logical containment. These attributes should be largely self-explanatory. There are so many of them that it’s soulcrushing to document each one! (Sorry ;) For further information please see: http://wiki.eve-dev.net/APIv2_Char_IndustryJobs_XML especially about completed_status and completed
